Long Tail Knits is proud to annouce the new partnership with Kris Jenkins of alk&me. She will be teaching the spinning and macrame workshops. She will also be providing all the fibers for spinning including rovings, batts and locks that you can use to create some wonderful yarns. There is a large selection of fibers for needle felting, too. We are also carrying all the supplies needed for macrame. Cording, rings, beads and some other items for the craft. Please join with me in welcoming Kris to the Long Tail Knits family!
